Aram

Unlike Summoner's Rift, Aram is only one lane where two teams fight each other. In this mode, you do not get to choose your champion. Instead, the game chooses five random champions for both you and the enemy team. In the game mode, each player gets two rerolls. Rerolls allow you to roll again for another champion that you own, or free champ (weekly free champs). In this game, you can also buy your team a skin boost. A skin boost grants players on your team a one-game only skin, chosen at random for the champion you are playing.
