Twisted Treeline

Twisted Treeline is completely different from the two other maps. In this map, there are three lanes: Top, Jungle, an Bot. This is different because there are only a total of 6 players on the map at the time. A 3v3 game mode. Both teams can choose a champion and also ban three champions. There is also two altars that anyone could capture. Capture one, you get a small buff, but capture two, the team gets a bigger buff.
