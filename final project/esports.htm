Esports in League of Legends

League of Legends is a very popular in esports with over 27 million playing the game daily and 67 million playing the game every month. League of Legends brings their community together by holding conventions and having tournaments. Every year, League of Legends hosts a World Championship Tournament, or Worlds. Teams from each reagion, NA, EU, Korea, China, Taiwan, Hong Kong, and Macao, compete with other teams originating from each of their own region face off to play in Worlds. The top 5 teams in their region attend worlds then play up until finals, the big stage.
