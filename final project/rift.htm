Summoner's Rift

Summoner's Rift is the main map of League of Legends and is the main part of the game. Two teams of five fight each other until one claims victory by destroying the enemy team's nexus, their base. To get to their nexus, each player must fight opponents that will protect it along with turrets standing in the way. You must destroy these turrets in order to siege victory. The key to winning game is to gain objectives and control your lane. In each lane, there are minions that spawn for the player to gain gold. With enough gold, you can buy items to achieve greater power. When you enter into a match of League, you can choose five roles, each with their own unique playstyle. In this map, there are 4 different game modes that you can play.

Top Lane

Top lane is where primarily bruisers and tanks in the game lane. In this lane, you are the sole player on your team that is in control of that lane. You and the enemy player duke each other out to win the lane. Remember, this is a team game!

  • Aatrox, The Darkin Blade
  • Darius, The Hand of Noxus
  • Bard, The Wandering Caretaker
  • Kled,The Cantakerous Cavalier
  • Rek'Sai, The Void Burrower
Middle (Mid) Lane

Middle Lane is where mages and assassins lane. Here is where the main focus of the game focuses on. This is the lane where the so called "Captain " of the team lanes. Just like top lane, this is a 1v1 situation. Best of Luck!

  • Ahri, The Nine-Tailed Fox
  • Ryze, The Rune Mage
  • Fiddlesticks, The Harbringer of Doom
  • LeBlanc, The Deceiver
  • Yasuo, The Unforgiven
Bottom (Bot) Lane

Unlike both top and mid lanes, bot lane is different. This is the lane where two members of your team work together. There are two categories in the bot lane. One role is the ADC, or the Attack Damage Carry, and the second role is Support, or Sup. The adc is primarily marksmen champions and the support is usually your tank champions. The adc's job is to farm minions and get kills in order to "carry " your team to victory.
